About Us

Where Content Meets Commerce

Founded in 2017 in Shanghai, DIAMIND MEDIA is a premier influencer management agency specializing in food, fashion, and lifestyle content creators across China's leading social platforms.

We represent eight exclusive top-tier creators with a combined reach exceeding 65 million followers, alongside partnerships with over twenty additional industry talents.

Our approach combines strategic brand positioning with authentic creator storytelling, delivering campaigns that drive measurable business outcomes for global and domestic brands alike.
