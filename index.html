About Us

Where Content Meets Commerce

Founded in 2017 in Shanghai, DIAMIND MEDIA is a premier influencer management agency specializing in food, fashion, and lifestyle content creators across China's top social platforms — Weibo, WeChat, Xiaohongshu, Douyin, and Bilibili.

We represent 8 exclusive top-tier creators with a combined reach of over 65 million followers, and maintain partnerships with 20+ additional creators in the industry.

Our founder Peggy Gong is recognized as a pioneer in food influencer management, having shaped the industry's commercial landscape since its early days.

Peggy Gong — Founder & Managing Director

Former Philips China and BeiTai Kitchen marketing executive. Founded Qdor Communications in 2016, pioneering food influencer representation in China. Interviewed by China Cuisine association. Now leading DIAMIND MEDIA to connect brands with creators at scale.
