About Us

Where Content Meets Commerce

Cross-border digital marketing connecting US brands with China's creator economy.

Founded in 2017, DIAMIND MEDIA INC is a premier digital marketing and influencer advertising agency, incorporated in New York and operating across the US and China.

We specialize in connecting US businesses with China's vibrant digital ecosystem through data-driven influencer marketing, content production, and digital advertising campaigns across TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Xiaohongshu, and WeChat.

Our exclusive roster of eight top-tier creators commands a combined reach exceeding 65 million followers, alongside partnerships with twenty additional industry talents spanning food, fashion, travel, and lifestyle verticals.

We deliver measurable business outcomes for global and domestic brands through strategic creator collaborations, advanced data analytics, and premium content production capabilities.
